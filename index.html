Speech therapy assesses and treats speech disorders, communication problems, and swallowing deficits. It helps people develop or recover skills like comprehension, clarity, voice, fluency, sound production, and swallowing. Speech therapy can treat childhood speech disorders or adult speech impairments caused by stroke, brain injury, or other conditions.
